# Community Rules

These are the simple, everyday rules for getting along on this Holt instance. The operator may edit them. The full [Terms of Service](#) still apply.

1. **Be decent.** No harassment, hate, threats, or targeted abuse.
2. **No illegal content.** Don't share or coordinate anything illegal where the instance is hosted.
3. **Respect consent.** Don't share other people's private information or impersonate them.
4. **Keep it clean for the room.** Follow the topic and tone of each channel. No spam, floods, or unwanted self-promotion.
5. **Don't break the service.** No attempts to overload the instance, bypass limits, or hijack accounts.
6. **Mind the minors.** Nothing sexual involving minors, ever. This is a hard line.

Owners and moderators can remove messages, restrict, or ban accounts that break these rules. If something is wrong, tell a channel owner or the instance operator.
